🎓 KEY CONCEPTS (Before you start)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

XBRL (eXtensible Business Reporting Language):
  • Machine-readable XML format of financial data
  • Filed with 10-K/10-Q by most large companies
  • Very accurate (direct from company accounting system)
  • Fast to parse (just XPath queries)
  • Preferred source

PDF:
  • Human-readable official filing document
  • 100% coverage (every company files PDF)
  • Fallback when XBRL unavailable
  • Less accurate (OCR + regex extraction)
  • Source of truth for validation

Reconciliation:
  • Compare XBRL vs PDF extraction
  • Flag if difference > 5%
  • Use XBRL as primary source
  • Flag PDF-only items for manual review

XML Output:
  • All financial data structured & labeled
  • Includes confidence scores
  • Includes audit trail (sources, dates)
  • Includes flags for manual review
  • Ready for downstream analysis
